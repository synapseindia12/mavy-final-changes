    What's MAVY?

    Short for Maverick - an independent thinker - MAVY is a platform for Millenials to have their voices heard, opinions understood, and desires met.

    Among other things, MAVY is a virtual community created and managed by millennials that can tell you exactly what they're thinking and a research tool available to marketers who have something to say.

    What's in it for me?

    Share your voice. Tell your favorite brands exactly what you want.

    Besides the satisfaction of having your voice heard and being a part of a like-minded community, you get paid! The more you use MAVY, the stronger your voice will be. MAVY provides each of us the opportunity to earn badges for sharing our opinions, and let's us collect points that can be cashed out for cool prizes.
